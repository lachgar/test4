The 5th International Workshop on E-Learning & Innovative Pedagogies (E-LIP’20)


The International Workshop on E-Learning & Innovative Pedagogies (E-LIP-19) will be held in Tangier, Morocco between 23rd and 24th of October 2019. This workshop aims to provide a forum for academic, research and industrial collaboration on pedagogies and engineering education. Nowadays, new information technologies and communication have invaded us, more than ever necessary in all areas, namely in the field of education. These technologies open new ways of access to knowledge. This workshop serves as a multi-disciplinary forum to exchange information on education, research, development, and applications of all topics related to e-Learning and Innovative pedagogies. You are invited to submit your works to this Special Session.

· Semantic Web and Ontologies in e-Learning

· Massive Open Online Course(MOOC) and Small Private Online Course (SPOC)

· Intelligent Tutoring Systems
· Virtual Universities, Classrooms, and Laboratories
· Other Innovative and Advanced Research in eLearning
